What is Papyrus AI?

Papyrus AI is a local, private AI assistant that transforms your static documents into a living knowledge base. Powered by Google Gemini and LangGraph, it lets you chat with your own files, uncover contradictions across your notes, and extract action items — all without your data ever leaving your machine.

Everything your second brain needs

✨ Features Section

🗂️ Multi-format Support

Upload PDFs, Word docs, plain text, or Markdown files. Papyrus handles them all.

💬 Chat with your Docs

Ask natural language questions and get precise answers with source citations from your own documents.

🔍 AI-Powered Insights

Discover non-obvious connections between your notes, spot contradictions, and surface forgotten action items automatically.

🔒 100% Private & Local

Your documents never leave your machine. No cloud storage. No data sharing. Just you and your knowledge.

⚡ Blazing Fast Retrieval

Powered by ChromaDB vector search — finds the most relevant chunks from thousands of pages in milliseconds.

🧠 Persistent Memory

Close the app, come back tomorrow — Papyrus remembers everything you've ever uploaded.

Three agents. One brain.

⚙️ How It Works

📥

Step 1 — Ingest

Drop in your files. The Ingestion Agent parses, chunks, and embeds them into a local vector database instantly.

💬

Step 2 — Query

Ask anything. The Query Agent retrieves the most relevant chunks and sends them to Gemini 2.5 Flash for a precise, cited answer.

💡

Step 3 — Discover

Hit "Generate Insights". The Insight Agent scans your entire knowledge base and surfaces contradictions, connections, and action items you never noticed.
